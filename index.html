What we do

This cosy, old school pub is tucked away in Norfolk Building on Surrey Street. Strand’s best kept secret - until now - was recently renovated but retains the quirky charm long time patrons know and love. The Philosophy Bar is our place for a calm chat after lectures, a game of pool or draughts, or to sample some of the best whiskey in all our venues.

What we offer

We offer a range of delicious, healthy drinks and snacks for you to grab on the go or sit and admire the view.

The Coffee

The Shack Coffee

The Shed’s coffee is 100% Arabica!. Our beans are ethically sourced and our supplier is fair trade certified.

With beans sourced from both Costa Rica in Central America and Colombia in South America, we get both a nutty flavour and winey sweetness which make for a smooth, sweet espresso. Our coffee is full bodied with a balanced acidity, which means it’s strong enough to shine through the milk-based drinks we all know and love.

The Food

Baguettes

Freshly baked in the wee hours of the morning by our attentive suppliers whose commitment to quality ensures we get the freshest, tastiest and most filling baguettes come lunchtime. They're organic and low in calories!

Wraps

Fresh, soft and absolutely delicious! These sandwiches are made with love and the finest ingredients. They’re pure and simply sliced, just for you!!

Paninis

Who doesn’t love a hot, cheesy, saucy Panini? And boy, do we have some great paninis for you! Our suppliers aren’t joking when they say they’ve got a Panini for every taste bud. Try one of these great flavours and see for yourself.

Salads

All Shed salads are complete meals, bursting with good healthy flavour. You’re sure to make friends with these 750cc packed serving containers of goodness. Dressings are as fresh as the salads and served separately so you can add it yourself.
